1.1 名词 (Nouns)

名词用于表示人、事物、地点或抽象概念，例如： book, apple, Paris, happiness

例句：book, apple, Paris, happiness

练习

  1. I have a ________ on the table.
  2. ______ is the capital of France.
  3. Honesty is a valuable ________.
book / Paris / quality

1.2 代词 (Pronouns)

代词用于替代名词，例如：I, you, he, she
